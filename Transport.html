Transport - Getting Here

From Kuala Lumpur to Penang

By Car: 3.45 hours to 4 hours (362km)
By Train: Electronic Train Service (ETS) runs from Kuala Lumpur Central Station Butterworth Penang five times a day. It takes 4 hours per ride, depending on the number of sites between Kuala Lumpur and Penang. The ticket price is RM59.
By Bus: All buses travel along Highway 1 to Penang. Some buses stop on the way to Ipoh. Some buses are direct and there is only one toilet/food station along the way. The bus service from Kuala Lumpur to Penang is about 5 hours. The bus fare from Kuala Lumpur to Penang is at least MYR 35 and the VIP/Luxury bus fare is MYR 50.
