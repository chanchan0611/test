About Us

       Leisure Travel Holidays is a travel agency based in Penang, Malaysia. It was registered as Leisure Holiday Holding in 1997. It is a small and medium-sized enterprises focused on bringing tourist the best holiday experience in local area. It provide touring to local and foreign tourist whether in group or personal trip/individual. In the year 2000, it was rewarded the best travel agency of Malaysia Award and in the year 2013, it was featured in the world famous travel and lifestyle magazine, Best Travel.
